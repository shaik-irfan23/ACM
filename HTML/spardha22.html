Spardha 2K22

      VVIT Guntur ACM Student Chapter hosted an annual Tech Fest, SPARDHA on August 22nd and 24th. The tech events span two days and comprise two spot events and four major events. The goal of Spardha 2K22 is to offer a platform for students' overall growth. Association for Computing Machinery (ACM) and ten additional professional societies are encouraged by VVIT for its various student programmes. VVIT has two ACM chapters, VVIT-ACM-Women and VVIT-ACM-Guntur, within the Departments of CSE, IT, CIC, AID, CSM & CSO.

       Participants include the student body from Andhra Pradesh's 36 engineering colleges. This festival is attended by students from our college as well as students from other colleges.
