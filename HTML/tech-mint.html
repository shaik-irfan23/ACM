TECH-MINT        

 

VVIT Guntur ACM Student Chapter hosted a TECH-MINT on Friday, April 29, 2022, from 3:35 PM to 5:00 PM. This event was attended by our college’s first and third-year students.

Tech Mint is a crossword puzzle event where the questions are based on technical computer basics. It tests the individual’s knowledge of the basics.
