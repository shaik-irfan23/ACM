CODEATHON

Vasireddy Venkatadri Institute of Technology (VVIT), under VVIT Guntur ACM Student Chapter conducted the event “CODEATHON-PRO” on 13 september 2019 from 12.30pm to 3:30PM in  New Block.

CODEATHON-PRO is an event,this contest is purely based on coding. This competition will enriches your technical knowledge and improves your coding skills, which will enhances your chances of winning in any global-level Codeathon contest.

Here you will be given a “three” questions of all the levels [easy, medium, hard] where you are expected to complete the programs in any language within time by completing all the test cases given. Evaluation: Points will be given based on your performance in each program.

  • The events are open for the students of 2nd year, 3rd year,  4th year of CSE, IT branches.
  • The team size is 1.

A total of 195 teams participated in the competition. They were also made aware of the rules of the competition. Thereafter, the event is started.
