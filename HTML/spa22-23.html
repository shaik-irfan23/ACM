SPARDHA 2K23


VVIT Guntur ACM Student Chapter hosted an annual Tech Fest, SPARDHA on August 10th and 11th. The tech events span two days and comprise two spot events and four major events. The goal of Spardha 2K23 is to offer a platform for students' overall growth. Association for Computing Machinery (ACM) and ten additional professional societies are encouraged by VVIT for its various student programmes. VVIT has two ACM chapters, VVIT-ACM-Women and VVIT-ACM-Guntur, within the Departments of CSE, IT, CIC, AID, CSM & CSO. Participants include the student body from Andhra Pradesh's 36 engineering colleges. This festival is attended by students from our college as well as students from other colleges.



Code Hustle was a competitive coding event in which the participants are provided with a set of problems. The participants are awarded points based on the number of questions they solve and the number of test cases they pass. The problems ranged from easy to difficult which affects their overall final score. The participants have to solve most of the questions in a given 1 ½ hour time frame.

Tech Trek is a thrilling treasure hunt competition where participants decode clues to uncover different college locations. The team that successfully reaches the ultimate clue emerges victorious. The event features multiple rounds designed to narrow down the competing teams. There are 2 rounds in total. The teams who win the 1st round will be the only teams that pplay the 2nd one.

However, there's a twist – the images are deliberately blurred, making identification a fascinating puzzle.

    "Grab the Part" is a thrilling event with 20 pairs of participants competing simultaneously. They stand opposite each other, poised to react when the instructor calls body parts like "knees" or "eyes." They need to hold the part they are told by the instructor. They will continue to grab the parts until they are told to stop and grab the item placed in front of the participants..
