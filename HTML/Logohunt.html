LOGOHUNT        

Vasireddy Venkatadri Institute of Technology (VVIT), under VVIT Guntur ACM Student Chapter organised The event LOGOHUNT on 30th October 2021. The event was conducted in the New Block CSE Dept. .

 

The event is about finding the names of the companies from logos given.

This event consists of 2 rounds 

  • Round 1: Easily recognizable companies logos are given.
  • Round 2: After round 1 participants are filtered based on the points they obtain and in this round the logos given are difficult because the logos are of companies which are not so common.
